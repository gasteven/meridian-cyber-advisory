FAQ

Questions before you book a call

How is this different from hiring a security consultant?

Most consultants deliver a report and leave. Meridian is an ongoing advisory relationship — direct access to the same person every month, accountable for the roadmap actually getting implemented, not just documented.

What happens if we outgrow a fractional arrangement?

That's a good outcome. Retainers are month-to-month, so scaling up hours, moving to a heavier engagement, or transitioning to an internal hire when you're ready is a conversation, not a contract renegotiation.

Do you sign an NDA?

Yes. A mutual NDA is standard before any detailed discovery call, and the Master Service Agreement includes confidentiality terms covering the full engagement.

We already use Vanta, Drata, or a similar compliance tool — do we still need this?

Those tools automate evidence collection; they don't decide what your security program should actually cover or defend it to an auditor. Meridian works alongside your existing tooling rather than replacing it.

How quickly can we get started?

The free risk snapshot call can happen this week. If it's a fit, a written proposal follows within a few days, and onboarding typically starts within one to two weeks of a signed SOW.

Do you replace our IT or engineering team?

No — Meridian sets strategy, policy, and roadmap, and works with your existing team (or contractors) to implement it. Think of it as the security leadership layer your team doesn't currently have, not a replacement for it.
