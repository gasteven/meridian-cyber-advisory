What data we actually hold

As a fractional advisory practice, we deliberately keep the data footprint small. In plain terms: we don't store scanned domains, we don't run tracking scripts on our own site, and the free tools are built to need as little of your data as possible to work.

  • Contact form submissions — name, email, company, and message, handled through Netlify's form infrastructure. Used only to respond to the inquiry.
  • Free scan tool — checks a domain's public DNS and HTTP response headers in real time. The domain entered is not stored; only anonymized, aggregate statistics (like the overall distribution of grades) may be recorded, with no link back to who ran a scan.
  • Client engagement data — during a paid engagement, any sensitive documents, credentials-adjacent findings, or policy drafts are handled per the signed engagement agreement, shared through agreed-upon channels (never over unencrypted email for sensitive material), and retained only as long as the engagement requires.

Full detail on data handling is in the Privacy Policy.

Incident response commitment

In the event of an incident affecting client data handled during an engagement, affected clients will be notified within 72 hours of confirmation, consistent with the standard we recommend clients hold their own vendors to. This site currently has no user accounts or stored client data, which limits the practical blast radius of a website-level incident.
