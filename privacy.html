Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Plain-language note: this policy is written to be genuinely read, not just filed. If anything here is unclear, email gabrielszegi8@gmail.com and ask.

What this site collects

Meridian Cyber Advisory (meridian-cyber-advisory.netlify.app) collects the minimum data needed to run the site and respond to you:

  • Contact form submissions — name, email, company, and message, submitted through the site's contact form. These are processed via Netlify's form-handling service and used only to respond to your inquiry.
  • Free Security Grade tool — when you enter a domain, that domain is checked in real time (public DNS records and the site's own response headers). The domain you enter is not stored. Only anonymized, aggregate statistics — such as the overall distribution of grades across all scans — may be recorded, with no link back to who ran a given scan or which domain was checked.
  • Standard hosting logs — like most websites, the hosting provider (Netlify) may log basic technical data (IP address, browser type, timestamps) for security and reliability purposes. This site does not add its own analytics or tracking scripts.

Cookies

This site does not set its own cookies. Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts, which may log IP addresses as part of serving font files, consistent with Google's own privacy practices — no cookie is set for this purpose on this site.

How information is used

  • To respond to contact form submissions and scheduling requests.
  • To operate and improve the free Security Grade tool.
  • To understand aggregate, anonymized usage of the site's free tools.

Information submitted through the contact form is never sold, rented, or used for purposes beyond responding to your inquiry.

Third-party services

  • Netlify — hosting, form handling, and the site's serverless functions.
  • Google Fonts — web font delivery.

Your choices

You can use the free Security Grade tool without submitting any personal information. If you've submitted the contact form and want your information deleted, email gabrielszegi8@gmail.com and it will be removed from Netlify's form records.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the site or services change. Material changes will update the "Last updated" date above.
